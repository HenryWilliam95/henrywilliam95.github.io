About Me

Allow me to introduce myself, my name is Henry Crofts and I am currently studying Computer Games Programming at the University of Suffolk, graduating in 2019. I am based in a small rual town on the Norfolk & Suffolk border and have lived here since 2000.

I have always been interested in how software is made, specifically Video Games. After working outside for six years in 2016 I decided it was time I followed my passion and went to University to prepare for a career change.

I hope that this online space can give you an insight into my work and how I have developed over the years spent at the University of Suffolk.
